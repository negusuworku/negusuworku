About the Dashboard

Purpose and Objectives

This Infographics Dashboard for Data Sharing platform is designed to provide a centralized, easy-to-understand overview of public health surveillance and emergency data. The primary goal is to support decision-makers with clear, actionable insights and to make accurate information available for partner organizations so they can implement appropriate measures effectively.

The platform uses interactive infographics, charts, and visual summaries to simplify complex data, making it easy to interpret, share, and utilize for informed decision-making. It serves as an educational tool, a portfolio showcase, and a practical dashboard for data visualization enthusiasts.
